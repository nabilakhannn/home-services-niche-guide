## Idea A: Industrial Precision

**Design Movement:** Neo-Industrial / Contractor-Grade Modernism
**Core Principles:** Raw authority, precision data, zero fluff, built-for-tradespeople
**Color Philosophy:** White canvas with burnt orange (#D4622A) as the dominant action color — evoking heat, urgency, and energy. Charcoal (#1A1A1A) for text authority. Light warm gray (#F7F5F2) for section breaks.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-screen hero (text left, visual right). Data-heavy stat bars. Full-bleed section alternations.
**Signature Elements:** Bold oversized numbers (stat callouts), thick left-border accent lines in burnt orange, diagonal section dividers
**Interaction Philosophy:** Every CTA pulses subtly. Hover states reveal data. Scroll-triggered counters for stats.
**Animation:** Fade-up entrance animations, counter animations on stats, subtle parallax on hero
**Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display/headlines) + Inter (body) — industrial contrast

## Idea B: Clean Authority (CHOSEN)

**Design Movement:** Modern B2B SaaS meets Trade Authority
**Core Principles:** Trust through clarity, results-first messaging, professional warmth, mobile-first
**Color Philosophy:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) background creates clinical trust. Burnt orange (#D4622A) for all CTAs, highlights, and accents — warm, energetic, action-oriented. Dark slate (#0F172A) for headlines. Medium gray (#64748B) for body text.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric hero with left-aligned bold headline + right-side visual. Horizontal stat bar. Card-based services. Alternating left/right content sections.
**Signature Elements:** Burnt orange underline accents on key phrases, pill-shaped stat badges, thick orange left-border on testimonial cards
**Interaction Philosophy:** Buttons scale on hover, smooth scroll, sticky nav with CTA button always visible
**Animation:** Subtle fade-up on scroll, counter animations for stats, smooth transitions
**Typography System:** Sora (bold display headlines) + DM Sans (body) — modern, trustworthy, readable
